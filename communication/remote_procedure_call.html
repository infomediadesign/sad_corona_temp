Remote Procedure Call

RMI and RPC as an alternative to the message-oriented paradigm.

"Many distributed systems have been based on explicit message exchange between processes. However, the operations send and receive do not conceal communication at all, which is important to achieve access transparency in distributed systems [...] In a nutshell, the proposal was to allow programs to call procedures located on other machines." [1, p.  173]

[1] Steen, M. van, & Tanenbaum, A. S. (2017). Distributed systems (Third edition (Version 3.01 (2017))). Pearson Education.
