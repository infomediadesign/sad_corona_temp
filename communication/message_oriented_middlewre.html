Message Oriented Middlewre

Messages on a higher level of abstraction

Message queue systems use a queue for messages and include management functions to pass on control or content.

They also provide an asynchronous communication model. This means that the sender and recipient of the message do not have to interact with the message queue at the same time. Messages that are placed in the queue are stored until the recipient retrieves them.

Most messaging systems support both the Publisher/Subscriber pattern and the Message Queue model in their API.
